# UX Review — Panel and Resource Refinement

Status: Proposed for approval  
Updated: 2026-07-23

## Findings

- Corrected B now changes only alignment: the controls remain horizontal and begin at the cover edge.
- Image thumbnails make otherwise similar imported image rows identifiable before removal. Inline expansion supports closer inspection without creating another layer.
- Cascade confirmation belongs to the primary group. Keeping affected child rows visible makes the consequence concrete; hiding the original Remove trigger prevents duplicate destructive actions.
- Media and Import Info behave as two states of one auxiliary workspace. A sticky internal switch keeps both reachable even when the panel is scrolled.
- Switching panels cancels pending deletion but preserves completed changes, Media selection/expansion, panel scroll, and the underlying recipe position.

## Remaining decisions

- Choose A or corrected B.
- Approve or revise thumbnail density and inline cascade confirmation after visual inspection.
