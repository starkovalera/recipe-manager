# UX review

Status: pass with one future validation item.

- The mode/resource split prevents Media and Import Info from reading as recipe modes.
- Persistent conditional Media removes the prior panel-to-panel navigation problem.
- Overlay behavior at 1280/1024 protects the reading task from severe compression.
- Ignored resources are findable without losing their parent-source relationship.
- Secondary deletion now requires a local confirmation without losing the selected row or thumbnail.
- The 1360 px transition behavior is approved; no further structural comparison is needed in low fidelity.
- Recipe deletion is reachable but de-emphasized in overflow, and its confirmation states the full consequence before action.
- Success exits the deleted object; failure preserves context and offers a concrete retry path.
