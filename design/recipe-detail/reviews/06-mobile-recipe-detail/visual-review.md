# Visual review

Status: internal pass; user approval pending.

Inspected nine deterministic screenshots at 360 and 390 px, including long title, both Focus tabs, Media, Import Info, inline resource confirmation, and recipe deletion.

- Header, actions, review status, and metadata maintain a clear compact hierarchy.
- The review status is proportional to its short message instead of spanning the viewport as a banner.
- Sheets use equal width, a consistent handle/header/close treatment, and recognizable media thumbnails.
- Icon-only close and trash controls remain visually distinct; no text Close buttons remain.
- A no-preview resource row now keeps the trash action square rather than stretching it across the row.
- Long titles wrap without hiding the action rows; difficulty/rating no longer leaves a separator stranded on a new line.

Remaining risk: typography, color, illustration quality, and final icon geometry are intentionally low-fidelity and must be resolved in the later visual-design phase.
