# UX Review — Visual-Control Refinement

Status: Proposed for approval  
Updated: 2026-07-23

- A and corrected B now differ only by the origin of the action/status rows; the warning remains equally compact.
- Cross and trash icons no longer overload one symbol with close and delete meanings.
- Cascade confirmation first protects the saved recipe, then describes exactly which imported materials disappear.
- A stable right consequence column keeps `Will be removed` and `Kept as cover` scannable across short and long labels.
- Removing the persistent panel switch prioritizes Media in Cooking Focus. Rare Import Info access remains available in overflow, with contextual return.
- External cooking links are visible where they are useful without importing lifecycle controls into Media.

Remaining decision: keep both header variants until high-fidelity typography and imagery are available.
